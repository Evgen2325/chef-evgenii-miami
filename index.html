Questions

Clear answers before you book.

Does $850/week include everything?

For the starter example, yes: $850/week is a turnkey starting plan for two people, lunch and dinner, five days per week, with standard groceries, cooking, packaging, and agreed delivery. Wagyu, lobster, caviar, rare ingredients, extra portions, long-distance delivery, and special requests are quoted separately.

Do I choose every dish?

For weekly meal delivery, no: Chef Evgenii proposes a balanced weekly menu based on your profile. For an on-site private chef day, you can choose preferred dishes from the menu and the final combination is confirmed after consultation.

Is the menu shown on this page available for delivery?

The dish menu is specifically for on-site private chef bookings, when Chef Evgenii cooks at your home. Weekly delivery uses a separate chef-led menu designed around your restrictions, favorite flavors, portions, and schedule.

Where do you deliver?

Miami, Broward, Boca Raton, and selected South Florida areas depending on schedule and distance.

How do payments work?

After consultation and quote approval, you receive a secure payment link or invoice. This site is ready for payment links but does not collect card data directly.
